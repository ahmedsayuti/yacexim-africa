About YACEXIM Africa

A dynamic agribusiness and commodities trading company strategically positioned within Africa's growing agricultural value chain, delivering quality food products to local and international markets while supporting sustainable farming and rural livelihoods.

Our Company

A subsidiary of YAC GROUP Holdings, driving agricultural transformation in Africa

Leading African Agribusiness & Export-Import Company

YAC EXIM AFRICA LTD is a dynamic agribusiness and commodities trading company operating as a subsidiary of YAC GROUP Holdings. We are strategically positioned within Africa's growing agricultural value chain, delivering quality food products to local and international markets while supporting sustainable farming and rural livelihoods.

Our operations span importation, export, production, and agricultural support services, with a strong focus on quality assurance, ethical sourcing, and long-term partnerships.

With deep expertise in African and international commodity markets, we bridge continents through agricultural trade, empowering farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture, and contributing to Africa's economic growth through efficient trade and innovation.
