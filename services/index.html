Our Agribusiness Services

Comprehensive agricultural value chain solutions from farm to market, delivering sustainable and efficient agribusiness services across Africa.

End-to-End Agricultural Solutions

YACEXIM AFRICA LTD offers comprehensive agribusiness services designed to optimize every stage of the agricultural value chain. From farming and sourcing to processing, export, and import logistics, we provide integrated solutions that enhance productivity, ensure quality, and create sustainable value for all stakeholders.

Our Comprehensive Services

Specialized solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of modern agriculture

Import & Export Services

Strategic importation of essential food items to support food security and export of premium African agricultural commodities to global markets.

Our Import Services:

  • Importation of food staples and agricultural inputs
  • Supply chain management for imported goods
  • Quality assurance and compliance with import regulations
  • Warehousing and distribution solutions

Our Export Services:

  • Export of raw cashew nuts, cocoa beans, and other commodities
  • International market access and trade facilitation
  • Export documentation and compliance
  • Logistics and shipping coordination

Commercial Farming & Support Services

Large-scale agricultural production with modern farming techniques and sustainable practices across multiple crop varieties.

Our Farming Services:

  • Large-scale crop production and farm management
  • Modern irrigation and precision farming techniques
  • Soil testing and fertility management
  • Crop rotation and sustainable land management

Farmer Support Services:

  • Agricultural inputs supply (seeds, fertilizers, pesticides)
  • Farmer training and capacity building
  • Extension services and technical support
  • Market linkage and price negotiation

Processing & Value Addition

Transforming raw agricultural products into value-added goods including cashew kernels, cocoa products, and processed foods.

Our Processing Services:

  • Cashew nut processing into various grades of kernels
  • Cocoa bean processing into butter, powder, and liquor
  • Grain milling and processing
  • Packaging and labeling services

Value Addition:

  • Product development and innovation
  • Quality grading and standardization
  • Brand development for processed products
  • Shelf-life enhancement techniques

Animal Husbandry & Dairy Production

Specialized livestock management, dairy farming solutions, and value-added dairy products for domestic and export markets.

Our Animal Husbandry Services:

  • Livestock breeding and management
  • Animal nutrition and feed formulation
  • Veterinary services and health management
  • Modern housing and welfare standards

Dairy Production:

  • Milk collection and processing
  • Dairy product development (cheese, yogurt, butter)
  • Quality control and food safety compliance
  • Cold chain management

Supply Chain Management

End-to-end logistics, storage, and distribution solutions ensuring quality preservation and timely delivery.

Our Supply Chain Solutions:

  • Logistics and transportation management
  • Warehousing and storage facilities
  • Inventory management and tracking
  • Cold chain logistics for perishables

Quality Preservation:

  • Pest control and fumigation services
  • Quality monitoring throughout the supply chain
  • Traceability systems implementation

Value Chain Development:
